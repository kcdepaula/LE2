Vegetarian Rice

Ingredients

  • 1/2 chopped onion

  • 1 chopped garlic clove

  • 1 chopped courgette

  • 1 grated carrot

  • 1 chopped red or yellow pepper

  • 75g frozen peas

  • 1/2 tin of chopped tomatoes

  • 350ml Vegetable stock

  • 150g Dried brown rice

  • 1 tbsp of dried parsley

  • 1 tsp turmeric

  • Seasoning

    Method

  1. Serves 2: Prep time 10 mins: cook time 25 mins

  2. 1) Add onion, garlic, and trumetic to pan with a splash of veg stock.

  3. 2) Stirring, cook gently for a 3 mins then add the chopped courgette, grated carrot and chopped pepper

  4. 3) Stir well for a further 3 mins then add the dried rice

  5. 4) Stir rice in well with vegetables

  6. 5) Once rice is distributed well throughout the mixture add the stock - leave to boil

  7. 6) Once boiled immediately turn heat down to simmer and cover. Cook for 10 mins

  8. 7) Add in chopped tomatoes and frozen peas and stir

  9. 8) Cover and simmer for a further 10 mins

  10. 9) Once cooked stir in dried parsley with seasoning

  11. 10) Spoon rice into 2 dishes
