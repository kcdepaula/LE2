Beef in Beer

Ingredients

  • 500g of cheap beef pieces (stewing steak usually is cheap)

  • 500 mls of real ale

  • 4 large onions

  • 2 fat cloves of garlic

  • tbsp of plain flour

    Method

  1. 1) Raw beef, roll it in the flour...fry 4-5 pieces at a time in a hot saucepan to seal...put to one side when all done.

  2. 2) Cute the onions into quarters...fry in the same frying pan as the beef...to soak up the juices...

  3. 3) Toward the end of frying the onion (they shoud be nicely brown round the edges) throw in the smashed up cloves of garlic.

  4. 4) Transfer the beef, onions, garlic to a deep casserole dish. Stir in the remaining flour.

  5. 5) Pour on the bottle of beer - really, it isn't going to be wasted!!!

  6. 6) Cover, and cook at 150 for about 2 and a half hours...will be gorgeus when it comes out, trust me...serve with mash
