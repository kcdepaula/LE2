Mission

Our mission is to provide the basic needs of those, who can not provide for themselves (or are not provided for by other means) and help them to become self-reliant and lead a decent life in dignity. The goal is, that these communities can continue to provide for their own population and children in health and education.

Vision

Our vision is to end the poverty in the country by 2040. Vision states that by 2040 the Philippines will be a prosperous, predominantly middle-class society where no one is poor.
